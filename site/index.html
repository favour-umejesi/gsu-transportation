Weekly Local Shuttle Runs

Free Rides for GSU Students

Free weekly rides offered exclusively to Grambling State University students, with stops at Walmart, local banks, the Ruston DMV, and Ruston Marketplace.

Be prepared to show the operator a valid GSU student ID when boarding.

Airport & Break Travel

Airport shuttle schedule

Free airport transportation for Grambling State students at the start and end of each term. Seats are limited, so reserve before the deadline for each trip.

Trip Travel window Reserve by Reservation
Fall 2026 Return to Campus Shuttle to campus for the start of the semester August 7 to 12, 2026 July 24, 2026 Reserve a seat
Winter Break Departure Shuttle home for Winter break travel November 18 to 23, 2026 November 11, 2026 Reserve a seat

Open to enrolled GSU students with a valid student ID. Seats fill quickly, so reserve as early as possible.
