Office of Student Transportation

Who We Are

The Office of Student Transportation keeps the Grambling State University community moving safely and reliably. We provide free, dependable transportation built around the needs of our students.

Our Mission

The Office of Student Transportation is committed to supporting the wellbeing and success of every student by providing safe, reliable, and accessible transportation. We serve as a vital resource for students without personal vehicles, ensuring access to essential destinations, fostering equity, and enhancing the student experience through clear communication and responsive service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a model of dependable, student centered transportation that empowers students to thrive academically, personally, and socially, while strengthening the connections between campus and community.

What We Stand For

Core Values

The commitments that guide every route we run and every rider we serve.

Safety

Prioritizing the protection and wellbeing of every passenger, on every trip.

Accessibility

Keeping transportation available and equitable for every student, without barriers.

Reliability

Delivering consistent, dependable service that students can plan their day around.

Communication

Sharing clear, timely, and transparent information about routes and service.

Student Success

Supporting academic and personal growth that reaches well beyond the classroom.

Community Connection

Linking students to the people, resources, and places that matter on and off campus.

Where We're Headed

Goals & Objectives

The priorities guiding the road ahead.

Enhance Student Wellbeing
Provide transportation to essential services such as healthcare, grocery stores, and other vital resources.
Maintain Operational Excellence
Uphold safety standards, punctuality, and ongoing staff training across the fleet.
Promote Communication & Transparency
Keep students informed about routes, schedules, and service updates in real time.
Foster Equity & Inclusion
Serve as a dependable resource for students without access to a personal vehicle.
