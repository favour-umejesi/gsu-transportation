Office of Student Transportation

Who We Are

We keep the Grambling State community moving safely, reliably, and for every student. Here is what drives the Office of Student Transportation.

“

Our Mission

The Office of Student Transportation at Grambling State University is committed to supporting the wellbeing and success of every student by providing safe, reliable, and accessible transportation. We serve as a vital resource for students without personal vehicles, ensuring access to essential destinations, fostering equity, and enhancing the overall student experience through clear communication and responsive service.

“

Our Vision

To be recognized as a model of dependable, student centered transportation services that empower students to thrive academically, personally, and socially, while strengthening connections between campus and community.
